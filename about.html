About

What is crochet?

Crochet is a craft of making textiles by interlocking loops of yarn or thread with a single hooked tool, called a crochet hook, creating fabric for items like blankets, clothes, and toys. Unlike knitting, which uses two needles, crochet forms each stitch individually before moving to the next, making it a versatile technique for intricate designs, from delicate lace to sturdy garments, using various materials like yarn, cotton, or even metal.

Granny square

You’ll be able to combine beautiful colours to stitch this traditional crochet design, perfect for setting you up for so many other projects. The kit even features a pattern for a granny square blanket to help you continue your adventure in crochet!

Crochet kits

A crochet kit is a convenient package containing all the necessary materials and instructions to complete a specific crochet project, like an amigurumi toy, blanket, or scarf, making it ideal for beginners or experienced crafters who want a stress-free experience by having yarn, patterns, hooks, and other tools (like needles, stuffing) bundled together.
